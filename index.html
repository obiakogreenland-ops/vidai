FAQ

Common questions.

What are credits?
Each AI request (a message or a video prompt generation) costs 1 credit. Free accounts start with 20. Credits never expire and can be purchased in packs. Pro and Studio plans have unlimited requests.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your conversations are stored locally in your browser by default. Your Anthropic API key is proxied through our secure backend — it's never exposed in the client. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt.
Does VidAI actually edit my videos?
VidAI is an AI advisor, not an NLE. It gives you expert-level scripts, shot lists, color recipes, and audio guidance that you implement in your own editor (Premiere, DaVinci, Final Cut). Think of it as a senior producer in your pocket.
Which video generators does it support?
The Video Generator crafts optimized prompts for Kling 3.0, Sora 2, Veo 3.1, and Seedance via Nano Banana. One click opens your prompt directly in the right tool.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, absolutely. Cancel from the billing portal inside the app with no questions asked. Your credits and history remain accessible until your billing period ends.
What's the daily streak for?
Consistent creators make better content. The streak tracks your daily visits and encourages regular use — the same way pros show up to their edit suite every day, not just when inspiration strikes.
